Information






Types.GravityTypes.Meaning


NoGravity
    No gravity field



UniformGravity
    Gravity field is described by a vector of constant gravity acceleration



PointGravity
    Central gravity field. The gravity acceleration vector is directed to
        the field center and the gravity is proportional to 1/r^2, where
        r is the distance to the field center.
