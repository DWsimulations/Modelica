Modelica.Media.Air.SimpleAir

Air: Simple dry air model (0..100 degC)

Information


                              
Simple Ideal gas air model for low temperatures

                              
This model demonstrats how to use the PartialSimpleIdealGas base class to build a
                              simple ideal gas model with a limited temperature validity range.
